About Us

JCI Properties is a relationship-driven commercial real estate firm specializing in industrial and office advisory in Austin, San Antonio, Houston and Dallas. We focus on long-term partnerships, strategic thinking, and trusted execution.

Jordan Walker

Founder & Managing Partner

Jordan is the Founder and Managing Partner of JCI Prop, bringing nearly 20 years of commercial real estate experience across Texas. He has worked with industry-leading firms including NAI Partners, HPI Real Estate Services & Investments, and CBRE, and is known for cultivating long-standing, loyal relationships with high-end clients built on trust, consistency, and results. Prior to entering commercial real estate, Jordan served as a Legislative Aide in the Texas House of Representatives for Rep. Charlie Geren of Fort Worth. That experience shaped his relationship-driven approach to business and gave him firsthand exposure to the intersection of policy, enterprise, and leadership alongside some of the state’s most respected decision-makers. Jordan began his brokerage career at NAI Partners in Houston, representing both owners and users in Office and Retail transactions. He later joined HPI, where he specialized in Industrial and Creative Office Leasing, primarily representing ownership. During his tenure, he was part of a team that leased more than 11 million square feet of space, earned Heavy Hitter recognition starting his first year, and ultimately led HPI’s San Antonio office while continuing leasing efforts throughout Austin and the I-35 corridor. At JCI Prop, Jordan applies this experience with a clear focus: delivering strategic real estate solutions while building enduring relationships—one square foot at a time.

Claire Wheeler

Chief Marketing Officer

Claire Wheeler serves as Chief Marketing Officer of JCI Prop, bringing nearly two decades of experience in brand strategy, marketing leadership, and business development. She has built and executed marketing initiatives for a wide range of companies, from entrepreneurial startups to luxury and high-end brands, with a consistent focus on positioning, storytelling, and long-term growth. Throughout her career, Claire has freelanced and consulted for businesses across multiple industries, helping them clarify their message, elevate their brand presence, and connect meaningfully with their target audiences. As a business owner herself, she brings a deep understanding of both the creative and operational sides of marketing, allowing her to align brand strategy directly with business objectives. At JCI Prop, Claire oversees brand development, marketing strategy, and client-facing communications, ensuring the firm’s identity reflects the same level of integrity, sophistication, and relationship-driven approach that defines its brokerage services. Her work is rooted in the belief that strong brands are built through clarity, consistency, and authentic connection.
